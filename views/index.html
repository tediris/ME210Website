About Us

We are Team Dogecoin, a group of three sophomores and one Co-Term. And we built a little robot that packs quite a lot.

The Project

Mechanical Design

The Millenium Doge features a multi-layer build that separates different subsystems, such as drive, sensors, actuators, coin collection, and electronics

Circuit Design

A triple infrared beacon sensor, variants of other beacon sensor circuits, and a vinyl tape sensor were all designed as part of the subsystem stage

Software

A set of software alogirthms were implemented to allow navigation of the robot to the BotCoin server, and from there to the desired BotCoin exchange, dumping off the collected coins
